About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at the Graduate School of AI, Seoul National University (HCC Lab). My research focuses on human–computer interaction and human–AI interaction, emphasizing accessibility enhancements and conversational agent design to create inclusive, user-centered experiences.
