About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University's Graduate School of AI, working in the Human-Centered Computing Lab (HCCL).

My research explores the evolving relationship between people and AI, working at the intersection of human-computer interaction (HCI) and human-AI interaction (HAI). I am interested in how AI mediates and reshapes human experiences, and how people perceive and relate to AI as a social entity in their everyday lives. My current work spans AI-mediated content experiences, conversational interaction, and accessible technology.

I'm always open to interdisciplinary collaboration and welcome opportunities to work with researchers, designers, and developers who share a passion for building more human-centered AI.
